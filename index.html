Why book with us

Whole homes, booked direct, hosted by people who live nearby and care about your stay.

Space for everyone

Big homes with multiple bedrooms and living areas, so the whole group stays together instead of scattered across rooms.

Book direct, save more

Deal directly with the owners — no platform service fees added on top, and a straight answer to your questions.

Local hosts & easy check-in

Self check-in, fast Wi-Fi and full kitchens as standard — plus local tips from hosts who know the area well.

A warm welcome, included

Every Maka stay begins with a complimentary gift basket of drinks and snacks waiting for you on arrival — and the kitchen comes stocked with the basics you always need: cooking oil, butter, salt and pepper, tea and coffee. Just arrive, settle in, and put the kettle on.

Where you'll find us

Two of the North Island's best holiday corners — the rolling Waikato countryside near Tīrau and Hobbiton, and the quiet Bay of Plenty coast at Ohiwa.

Both within easy reach of Hamilton, Tauranga and Rotorua, and both perfect for a big group getaway — a family reunion, a milestone birthday, or just the whole crew away together.
